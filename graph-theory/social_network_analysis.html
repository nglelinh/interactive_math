Graph Representation: \(G = (V, E)\) where \(V\) = users, \(E\) = relationships

Adjacency Matrix: \(A_{ij} = 1\) if there's a connection between users \(i\) and \(j\)

Degree Centrality: \(C_D(v) = \frac{deg(v)}{|V|-1}\)

Betweenness Centrality: \(C_B(v) = \sum_{s \neq v \neq t} \frac{\sigma_{st}(v)}{\sigma_{st}}\)
