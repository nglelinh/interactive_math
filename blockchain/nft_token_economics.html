🎨 NFT & Token Economics Mathematics

Non-Fungible Tokens & Economic Models

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) represent unique digital assets with verifiable ownership on blockchain networks. The mathematics behind NFTs involves rarity calculations, pricing models, and economic mechanisms.

Token Economics studies the economic incentives and mathematical models that govern cryptocurrency and token systems, including distribution, inflation, and utility mechanisms.

🧮 NFT & Token Mathematics

Rarity Score: RS = Σ(1 / trait_frequency) for all traits

Floor Price: FP = min(active_listings)

Market Cap: MC = floor_price × total_supply

Token Velocity: V = GDP / M (where GDP = transaction volume, M = money supply)

Bonding Curve: Price = supply^n (where n > 1 for exponential growth)
